Deeper Dive: Regular vs. Irregular Verbs üß†

Most verbs are easy, but some are totally random! Here's how to remember them:

Regular Verbs: The Easy Ones ‚ú®

  • Add `-ed` to most verbs: play -> played, watch -> watched
  • If it ends in `-e`, just add `-d`: like -> liked
  • If it ends in a consonant + `-y`, change `-y` to `-i` and add `-ed`: study -> studied
  • If it's a short word with a consonant-vowel-consonant (CVC) pattern, double the last letter and add `-ed`: stop -> stopped

Irregular Verbs: The Bosses üëë

These verbs don't follow the rules. You just have to memorize them! Here are a few to get you started:

  • go -> went
  • do -> did
  • see -> saw
  • have -> had
  • sleep -> slept
